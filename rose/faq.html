FAQ

Is there a return policy?

There is not a return policy. There is not a return policy because all orders are custom made! The only way in which we are able to take returned shoes is if they are damaged when they arrive at the designated location.

How long is the usual wait for custom shoes?

The wait is usual around 1-2 weeks because of all of the orders coming in. It also adds another week if there is extensive detail on the shoes.
